Indexing terms

The Indexer compiles all term candidates identified by the Spotter to build a bank of terms. In the process it may combine several term candidates as one depending on the configuration.

The files containing the term candidates are found in an input directory as exported by the Spotter in XMI format.

There is a lot of configuration possibilities to control the way the term candidates are combined as well as the export of the compiled and combined terms. You can explore them below.

Once you have filled the various parameters, you can click on the Run button on the top left of this window to launch the processing.
